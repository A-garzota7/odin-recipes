Lunchables Pizza

This is the correct way of making a lunchables pizza.

Ingredients

  • A microwave safe plate
  • A 900 watt microwave oven
  • Either a lunchables cheese pizza or a Lunchables pepperoni pizza package

Steps

  1. Place the three pieces of flatbread on your plate
  2. Place an even amount of marinara sauce across the three pieces of bread
  3. Apply equal parts of mozzarella cheese over the sauced bread
  4. Repeat with other cheese or pepperoni, as applicable
  5. Place plate of pizzas inside microwave and set it to one minute for the cheese variation and 50 seconds for the pepperoni counterpart
