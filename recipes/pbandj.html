Peanut butter and jelly sandwich

This is a simple peanut butter and jelly sandwich recipe.

Ingredients

  • Peanut Butter
  • Grape or Strawberry jelly
  • 2 Slices of bread

Steps

  1. Spread 1 Tbsp of Peanut butter across one slice of bread
  2. Spread 2 tsp of Grape or Strawberry jelly across the other slice
